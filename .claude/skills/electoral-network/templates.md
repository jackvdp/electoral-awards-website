## 11. Winner Citation (75–120 words)

### Template

**[Award Name]**
**[Winner Name / Organisation]**
**[Country]**

[Winner] is recognised for [their initiative/achievement]. [1–2 sentences describing what they did, the context, and the challenge they addressed.] [1 sentence on the impact or results achieved.] [1 sentence on why this is significant for the broader electoral community.]

### Example

**Election Management Award**
**Antigua and Barbuda Electoral Commission**
**Antigua and Barbuda**

The Antigua and Barbuda Electoral Commission is recognised for its exceptional management of the 2024 General Elections despite significant logistical challenges facing small island developing states. The Commission implemented innovative voter registration technology and streamlined polling procedures that reduced wait times by 40%. Their approach offers a replicable model for electoral management in small island contexts.
