Dear esteemed colleague,

It is with great pleasure that we extend to you an invitation to participate in the 22nd International Electoral Awards & Symposium, jointly hosted by the International Centre for Parliamentary Studies (ICPS) and the Commission on Elections of the Philippines (COMELEC).

This distinguished event will take place in Manila, Philippines from Saturday, 29th November to Wednesday, 3rd December 2026. It will gather electoral leaders, professionals, and innovators from across Asia-Pacific and beyond to share knowledge, recognise achievements, and engage in high-level discussions on the future of electoral management.


Event Overview:

The five-day programme includes a cultural tour, two days of symposium sessions with keynote presentations, panel discussions, fringe demonstrations, and networking opportunities. The event will culminate in the International Electoral Awards Ceremony, honouring excellence and innovation in electoral administration.

A brief overview of the itinerary is provided below, and further details can be found in Appendix 1:

Saturday, 29th November: Arrival of delegates
Sunday, 30th November: Cultural tour and cultural night
Monday, 1st December: Symposium (Part 1)
Tuesday, 2nd December: Symposium (Part 2) and Awards Ceremony
Wednesday, 3rd December: Departures


Awards:

This prestigious event will recognise and celebrate the outstanding contributions made by individuals and organisations in the field of electoral affairs. The awards to be presented are as follows:

International Institutional Engagement Award
Electoral Conflict Management Award
Accessibility for All Award
Election Management Award
Citizens' Engagement Award
First Time Voter Award
Electoral Ergonomy Award
Lifetime Achievement Award
Electoral Commissioner of the Year
Electoral Commission of the Year

The nomination process will be opened shortly.
