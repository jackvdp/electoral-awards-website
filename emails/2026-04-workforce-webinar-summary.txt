ICPS Electoral Workforce Webinar

Proposed Date: Friday 17 April 2026
Duration: 2 hours
Platform: Zoom

Title: Empowering the Election Workforce: From Global Survey Findings to Blockchain Solutions

Description: ICPS present the results of a global electoral workforce survey spanning 26 jurisdictions across four regions. Drawing on responses from senior electoral officials worldwide, this webinar examines the operational challenges facing Election Management Bodies — from managing temporary workforces of up to 1.5 million staff, to training gaps, credential verification, and workforce retention between election cycles. The session then turns to solutions, with a deep dive into how blockchain technology can address the pain points identified by practitioners — from secure credential verification to streamlined workforce management. With perspectives from academia, the field, and technology partners, join us for an honest, data-led conversation about what electoral officials told us they need and the practical tools that could deliver it.
