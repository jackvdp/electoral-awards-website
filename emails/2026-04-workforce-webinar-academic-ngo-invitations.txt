Dear Dr Garnett,

I hope this message finds you well. My name is Jack Vanderpump and I am Head of Policy Research at the International Centre for Parliamentary Studies (ICPS), a research institution of the United Nations Public Administration Network.

I am writing to invite you to join us as a speaker at our upcoming webinar, "Empowering the Election Workforce: Global Survey Findings & Solutions on Recruitment, Training, and Retention," taking place on 24 April 2026 from 14:00 to 16:00 (GMT+1).

The session will present the findings of a global electoral workforce survey conducted by ICPS, spanning 26 jurisdictions across four regions. Drawing on responses from senior electoral officials worldwide, it examines the operational challenges facing Election Management Bodies — from managing temporary workforces of up to 1.5 million staff, to training gaps, credential verification, and workforce retention between election cycles.

Your work as Co-Director of the Electoral Integrity Project, and your research on election management, election technologies, and electoral administration, would bring a valuable comparative perspective to the discussion. We would be delighted to have you contribute your insights alongside other leading voices in this field.

I appreciate that this is relatively short notice, and completely understand if your schedule does not permit. If you are available, we would ask for a contribution of approximately 15–20 minutes, followed by a panel discussion and audience Q&A.

I would be very happy to share the full survey findings and further details about the session at your convenience.

With kind regards,
