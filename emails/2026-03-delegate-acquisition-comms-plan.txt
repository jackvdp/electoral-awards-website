NOTES

- Segment the database by region and engagement level for targeted
  messaging. Asia-Pacific contacts should receive additional emphasis
  given the Manila location.

- All emails should include the key selling points:
  • Free attendance
  • Accommodation covered at The Manila Hotel
  • 5-day programme including cultural tour and Awards Ceremony
  • Co-hosted with COMELEC
  • Networking with 150+ electoral leaders from around the world

- Social media should mix promotional posts with engagement content
  (polls, questions, throwbacks to previous years) to avoid being
  purely transactional.

- Phone outreach is most effective 1–2 weeks after an email campaign,
  when the event is fresh in recipients' minds.

- Track registration sources to understand which channels convert best
  and adjust resource allocation accordingly.
