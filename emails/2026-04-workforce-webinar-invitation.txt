We are pleased to invite you to the next Electoral Stakeholders' Network webinar:

Empowering the Election Workforce: Global Survey Findings & Solutions on Recruitment, Training, and Retention
Date: Friday, 10 April 2026
Time: 14:00–16:00 UTC
Duration: 2 hours
Platform: Zoom

The International Centre for Parliamentary Studies (ICPS) will present the results of a global electoral workforce survey spanning 26 jurisdictions across four regions. Drawing on responses from senior electoral officials worldwide, this session examines the operational challenges facing Election Management Bodies — from managing temporary workforces of up to 1.5 million staff, to training gaps, credential verification, and workforce retention between election cycles.

The session will also explore how emerging technologies, including blockchain, can address the pain points identified by practitioners, with perspectives from academia, the field, and technology partners.
